Room Types

Single Occupancy Room

A Single Occupancy Room (SRO) is a type of affordable housing where each tenant has their own private room but shares communal facilities like bathrooms and kitchens.

Family Room

A Family Room is a type of accommodation that is designed to comfortably house multiple members of a family.

Duplex Room

A Duplex Room is a type of hotel room or accommodation that spans two levels. This setup provides a unique living experience with additional space and a feeling of privacy similar to an apartment or townhouse.
